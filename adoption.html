Animal Adoption

Solo Project

Plotly dash app that predicts whether or not a dog or cat will be adopted. Completed in 1 week. After exploratory data analysis and data cleaning, a random forest classifier was used on data that was narrowed down to cats and dogs based on numerous features. In the Plotly Dash app, users are able to customize features of a particular animal and a probability is returned on the animal being adopted or not.
