Opti

Solo Project

Completed a data story telling project in 1 week. The objective was to determine a research question based on the dataset chosen and create explanatory visualizations to accompany finding after thorough data cleaning and data analysis.

This was an interesting topic because I enjoy history, and recently in particular the American Civil War, but also as my first data science project, an important lesson on the state of real life data. The dataset was historic battles but there was an excessive amount of missing values.

I sought out to answer if being on the offensive during a battle would result in fewer casualties than being on defense. I looked particularly at the American Civil and Revolutionary Wars and the more popular battles and determined casualties which were calculated by using both those there initially and reinforcements which were then normalized for the size of the armies so they could be compared.

Perhaps unsurprisingly, there are many factors that go into the loss of men during battle and it can not be concluded whether being on the attacking of defending side will ensure fewer losses, but it was an interesting look into history from a data science perspective.
