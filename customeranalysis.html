Quantitative Customer Analysis

Solo Project

4-week project as a Growth Data Analyst Intern for HMBradley, a Fintech startup. The objective of the project was to identify HMB's current customers in order to more efficiently target future customers.

In terms of soft skills, I worked with the Growth team as well as other members within the organization to develop the project scope. This was a great learning experience in itself because HMB had never conducted a customer analysis so it was important to narrow ideas that were specific enough to achieve and also realistic within the time frame. Another aspect of the project where I gained experience was in presenting both my project pitch as well as the final product in front of the CEO and other executives within the company.

On the technical side, the analysis was performed with Retool which uses postgreSQL. I gathered data from across multiple relational databases and combined into two tables, one for demographic data and another for behavioral which tracked the crucial milestones during the customer journey. From there, an interactive app was created that included visualizations for the demographic data to identify key customer groups. It also included cohort analsyses for those key milestones.

From a marketing and growth perspective, the insights derived from the analysis are invaluable. Increased efficiency with marketing campaigns to target those customers we know are successfully completing the journey would help to lower the CAC. Likewise, using the cohort analyses in conjunction with the marketing calendar to track the success of different marketing strategies and campaigns would allow the company to identify areas of improvement or when the best time to communicate with customers in order to get them to that next milestone.
